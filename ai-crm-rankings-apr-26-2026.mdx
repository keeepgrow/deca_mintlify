## What DECA Score, Mention Rate, and Rank Mean

DecaGEO measures three core metrics for every brand in every category.

### DECA Score (0 to 100)

DECA Score answers the question: how strongly does AI recommend this brand in this category? It combines how often a brand is mentioned with where it appears in the response. Brands recommended in the first position earn more points than brands mentioned later. The score is normalized so that 100 means the brand was recommended first in every single response.

### Mention Rate (percent)

Mention Rate is the percentage of AI responses that include a brand, regardless of position. A 93.7% Mention Rate means HubSpot was named in 93.7 out of every 100 CRM-related AI responses this week.

### Rank

Rank is the brand's overall position in the category, determined by DECA Score. Rank 1 in CRM means the brand has the highest DECA Score in CRM this week.

## How DecaGEO Measures AI CRM Rankings

DecaGEO's method differs from most AI visibility tools in three ways.

**Persona-driven queries, not generic prompts.** Most AI visibility tools ask AI generic questions like "what is the best CRM software?" DecaGEO designs queries from the buyer's perspective. Each query reflects a real buying context: team size, budget, primary use case. Over 100 buyer panels generate the queries that feed the weekly measurement.

**Recommendation-only counting.** DecaGEO only counts brands that AI recommends as a solution. Passing mentions, comparison filler, and negative references are excluded. A brand is only credited when AI is suggesting it as a choice for a buyer in that category.

**Same model, same week, every week.** AI responses vary across model versions and access methods. DecaGEO fixes its measurement to a single model per platform (currently ChatGPT, GPT-5.4) and a single weekly collection window (Sundays, Eastern Time). Week-over-week changes in DECA Score reflect real shifts in AI behavior, not measurement noise.

### What is the difference between DECA Score and Mention Rate?

Mention Rate measures how often a brand is mentioned in any position. DECA Score measures both how often the brand is mentioned and where it appears in the response. A brand mentioned often but always in last position will have a high Mention Rate but a lower DECA Score. A brand mentioned only when it is in first position will have a high DECA Score but a moderate Mention Rate. The two metrics together describe AI visibility more completely than either alone.
