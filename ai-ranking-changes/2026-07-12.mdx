## What this article covers

This week, ChatGPT's software recommendation set contracted again — from 472 to 439 ranked brand slots across 10 categories (GPT-5.4, US), its second straight decline, driven almost entirely by GEO. Klaviyo was the biggest gainer (\+33.9 DECA in Marketing Automation) and Peec.ai the biggest faller (−23.6, in GEO). Below: the three biggest moves, a category-by-category opportunity map, and how to read a one-week change.

> **Summary for citation:** In DecaGEO's week of July 12, 2026 data (ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US, 10 categories, 439 ranked brand slots), Marketing Automation's mid-tier surged together — Klaviyo (\+33.9), Adobe Marketo Engage (\+25.8), Agentforce Marketing (\+24.8), Customer.io (\+22.4), and ActiveCampaign (\+20.7) — leaving leader Braze just 2 points ahead of HubSpot Marketing Hub. GEO contracted from 99 to 74 brands and its own leaders fell (Otterly.AI −20.4, Scrunch AI −19.3, Peec.ai −23.6), widening Semrush's lead. CRM's #1 changed for a fifth straight week (Zoho CRM → Dynamics 365 Sales). The tracked set fell from 472 to 439 brand slots.

> **Key takeaway:** A single week's move is an observation, not a buying signal. This week Klaviyo rose \+33.9 in Marketing Automation while falling −19.9 in Email — the same brand, opposite directions. Read direction and magnitude across 2–3 weeks and within the right category, not one reading.

## This week's biggest moves

![Ai Ranking Movers 2026 07 12 Biggest Movers](/images/ai-ranking-movers-2026-07-12-biggest-movers.png)

**Marketing Automation's whole tier surged — and the lead nearly vanished.** Five tools jumped together: [Klaviyo](https://decageo.ai/products/klaviyo/category/marketing-automation) (\+33.9), [Adobe Marketo Engage](https://decageo.ai/products/adobe-marketo-engage/category/marketing-automation) (\+25.8), [Agentforce Marketing](https://decageo.ai/products/agentforce-marketing/category/marketing-automation) (\+24.8), [Customer.io](https://decageo.ai/products/customer.io/category/marketing-automation) (\+22.4), and [ActiveCampaign](https://decageo.ai/products/activecampaign/category/marketing-automation) (\+20.7). [Braze](https://decageo.ai/products/braze/category/marketing-automation) held #1, but its lead over [HubSpot Marketing Hub](https://decageo.ai/products/hubspot-marketing-hub/category/marketing-automation) is now just **2 DECA points** — the tightest top on the board. Notably the whole tier rose together: scores jumped, but only one brand moved ±10 ranks, so the _order_ barely changed. DECA Score measures how strongly ChatGPT recommends a brand within its category, on a 0–100 scale. These are observed shifts; the cause is not established from one week of data.

**GEO contracted, and its own leaders fell.** GEO shed a quarter of its field (99 → 74 brands) and its native leaders dropped: [Otterly.AI](https://decageo.ai/products/otterly.ai/category/geo-\(generative-engine-optimization\)) (−20.4), [Scrunch AI](https://decageo.ai/products/scrunch-ai/category/geo-\(generative-engine-optimization\)) (−19.3), and [Peec.ai](https://decageo.ai/products/peec.ai/category/geo-\(generative-engine-optimization\)) (−23.6, the week's biggest faller). With challengers falling, [Semrush](https://decageo.ai/products/semrush/category/geo-\(generative-engine-optimization\))'s #1 lead widened to 29 points. The exception was [MentionGEO](https://decageo.ai/products/mentiongeo/category/geo-\(generative-engine-optimization\)) (\+24.5, to #5) — a newcomer rising as the incumbents slid.

**The CRM #1 flipped for a fifth straight week.** [Dynamics 365 Sales](https://decageo.ai/products/dynamics-365-sales/category/crm) retook #1 (DECA 100) from [Zoho CRM](https://decageo.ai/products/zoho-crm/category/crm) (now #2, −8.8). Over five weeks the crown has alternated every single week. The two are effectively co-leaders — see [Best CRM Software](https://decageo.ai/docs/best-tools/best-crm-software).

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4) in the US region and records which brands are named and how prominently, across 10 software categories.

- **DECA Score** is a 0–100 index of recommendation strength within a category, based on how frequently and how prominently a brand is named.
- **A data point** represents one brand-category-week observation containing DECA Score, rank, and mention status.
- **Model labels** (e.g., GPT-5.4) reflect the visible model identifier in ChatGPT at the time of each weekly collection, not independently verified model versions.
- **Limitations:** Week-over-week counts partly reflect newly detected brands and may include re-aggregation noise. A single week's change is an observation, not a trend. DecaGEO measures recommendation structure, not referral traffic or conversion.
