## This week's biggest moves

**[Agentforce Marketing](https://decageo.ai/products/agentforce-marketing/category/marketing-automation) rose to #6 in Marketing Automation.** Salesforce's agentic marketing product gained 22.6 DECA points week over week — the largest single move in the index. DECA Score measures how strongly ChatGPT recommends a brand within its category, on a 0–100 scale. This is an observed shift; the cause is not established from one week of data.

**[HubSpot Sales Hub](https://decageo.ai/products/hubspot-sales-hub/category/crm) climbed in CRM.** Its DECA Score rose 16.1 points while it held rank #3, widening the gap over Agentforce Sales at #4. This continues the pattern documented in [HubSpot Sales Hub vs Agentforce Sales](/docs/best-tools/hubspot-vs-salesforce), where the two were tied for two weeks before HubSpot pulled ahead.

**Project Management is a dead heat at the top.** [monday Work Management](https://decageo.ai/products/monday-work-management/category/project-management) holds #1, but only 1 DECA point separates it from #2, and 8 of 54 brands moved ±10 ranks this week. For a category leader, a 1-point moat is a signal to watch challengers closely.

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4) in the US region and records which brands are named and how prominently, across 10 software categories.

- **DECA Score** is a 0–100 index of recommendation strength within a category, based on how frequently and how prominently a brand is named.
- **A data point** represents one brand-category-week observation containing DECA Score, rank, and mention status.
- **Model labels** (e.g., GPT-5.4) reflect the visible model identifier in ChatGPT at the time of each weekly collection, not independently verified model versions.
- **Limitations:** Week-over-week counts partly reflect newly detected brands and may include re-aggregation noise. A single week's change is an observation, not a trend. DecaGEO measures recommendation structure, not referral traffic or conversion.
