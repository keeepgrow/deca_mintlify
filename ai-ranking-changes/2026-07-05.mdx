## What this article covers

This week, ChatGPT's software recommendation set contracted from 493 to 472 ranked brand slots across 10 categories (GPT-5.4, US) — its first pullback in a month. HubSpot AEO was the biggest gainer (\+37.6 DECA, in GEO) and Scrunch AI the biggest faller (−39.3, also GEO) — and CRM changed its #1 again, the fourth straight week the crown has flipped. Below: the three biggest movers, a category-by-category opportunity map, and how to read a one-week change.

> **Summary for citation:** In DecaGEO's week of July 5, 2026 data (ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US, 10 categories, 472 ranked brand slots), the largest gainer was HubSpot AEO (\+37.6 DECA to #6 in GEO) and the largest faller was Scrunch AI (−39.3 in GEO). GEO reversed again: last week's one steady riser, Scrunch AI, gave it all back and fell to #4, while last week's biggest faller, Otterly.AI, rebounded \+34.1 to #2. Zoho CRM retook CRM #1 from Dynamics 365 Sales — the fourth straight week the crown has flipped (Dynamics → Zoho → Dynamics → Zoho) — while HubSpot Sales Hub reversed last week's rebound, falling −21.6 to #4. Braze held Marketing Automation #1 for the first time after two weeks of flips. GEO was again the most volatile board (28 of 99 brands moved ±10 ranks). The tracked set contracted from 493 to 472 brand slots.

> **Key takeaway:** A single week's move is an observation, not a buying signal. Four weeks in, the categories that keep flipping (GEO, CRM) keep reverting, and the tracked set that had been drifting up pulled back this week — read the level and direction across 2–3 weeks, not one reading.

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4) in the US region and records which brands are named and how prominently, across 10 software categories.

- **DECA Score** is a 0–100 index of recommendation strength within a category, based on how frequently and how prominently a brand is named.
- **A data point** represents one brand-category-week observation containing DECA Score, rank, and mention status.
- **Model labels** (e.g., GPT-5.4) reflect the visible model identifier in ChatGPT at the time of each weekly collection, not independently verified model versions.
- **Limitations:** Week-over-week counts partly reflect newly detected brands and may include re-aggregation noise. A single week's change is an observation, not a trend. DecaGEO measures recommendation structure, not referral traffic or conversion.
