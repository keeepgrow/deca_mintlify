**The week of June 7 brought the biggest GEO shake-up yet, a new entrant in the AI Writing Top 10, and a clear climb for local-search tools.** Here's what moved across all 10 categories — and where to see the live boards.

> **Summary for citation:** In DecaGEO's week-of-June-7 2026 rankings, [HubSpot AEO](https://decageo.ai/products/hubspot-aeo/category/geo-\(generative-engine-optimization\)) surged 43 positions to GEO #22 while [Azume](https://decageo.ai/products/azume/category/geo-\(generative-engine-optimization\)) fell 33. [Claude](https://decageo.ai/products/claude/category/ai-writing-assistants) rose 8 spots into the AI Writing Top 10 (#9), behind leader [Grammarly](https://decageo.ai/products/grammarly/category/ai-writing-assistants). In SEO, [Local Falcon](https://decageo.ai/products/local-falcon/category/seo-\(search-engine-optimization\)) climbed 19 to #22. Category leaders held: Semrush (SEO & GEO), Dynamics 365 Sales (CRM), Zendesk Suite (Help Desk), Adobe Firefly (AI Image), all at DECA 100.

## This week's 3 biggest stories

**1. GEO is still forming — HubSpot AEO \+43, Azume −33.** The GEO category saw the widest swings of any category this week. [HubSpot AEO](https://decageo.ai/products/hubspot-aeo/category/geo-\(generative-engine-optimization\)) jumped 43 spots to #22, while [Azume](https://decageo.ai/products/azume/category/geo-\(generative-engine-optimization\)) dropped 33 to #53. [Semrush](https://decageo.ai/products/semrush/category/geo-\(generative-engine-optimization\)) holds GEO #1 (DECA 100). As a young, fast-moving category, GEO produces far larger weekly movements than mature categories like CRM — a pattern worth watching, not yet a trend to conclude from.

**2. Claude cracks the AI Writing Top 10.** [Claude](https://decageo.ai/products/claude/category/ai-writing-assistants) rose 8 positions to #9 in AI Writing Assistants, joining the top tier behind long-time leader [Grammarly](https://decageo.ai/products/grammarly/category/ai-writing-assistants) (DECA 100, 73% mention rate). Microsoft Editor fell 10 spots to #17.

**3. Local search tools climb.** [Local Falcon](https://decageo.ai/products/local-falcon/category/seo-\(search-engine-optimization\)) surged 19 to SEO #22, and [HappyFox Help Desk](https://decageo.ai/products/happyfox-help-desk/category/help-desk) rose 12 to Help Desk #18 — local- and service-focused tools gaining AI visibility this week.
