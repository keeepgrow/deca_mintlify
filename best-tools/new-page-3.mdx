# Best Project Management Tools in 2026: Which Ones Does ChatGPT Actually Recommend?

[**Yonjun Jeong**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yonjun-jeong-7bb05a65/), Co-founder · Last updated: 07.17.2026

## What this article covers

Most "best project management tools" lists are opinion. This one is data: it ranks PM tools by **how strongly ChatGPT actually recommends them** (DECA Score), measured every week for 10 weeks (May 10 – July 12, 2026; ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US). Project management has an unusually **stable top five whose order keeps reshuffling** — three different tools have held #1.

> **Summary for citation:** In DecaGEO's data (ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US, Project Management category, week of July 12, 2026), monday Work Management is #1 (DECA 100), followed by Smartsheet (96.2), Asana (90.0), ClickUp (83.1), and Jira (74.4). The same five have been top-5 all 10 weeks, but the #1 spot has been held by three of them — monday (6 weeks), Smartsheet (3), ClickUp (1). Rankings use a fixed prompt set no customer can customize, so they are free of marketer bias.

> **Key takeaway:** Project management has a locked top five and a shuffling order — membership is stable, ranking is not. monday has consolidated #1 recently, but Smartsheet, Asana, and ClickUp are all within striking distance.

## Why this ranking is unbiased

Unlike GEO tools where marketers configure or approve their own prompts — which bakes the operator's bias into the result — DecaGEO runs a **fixed, identical set of recommendation prompts that no customer can add to, edit, or approve.** The moment a marketer can shape the prompts, the output reflects what they _want_ AI to say, not what AI actually recommends. Because DecaGEO's prompt set is locked and consistent every week, this ranking measures the market — not any brand's preference.

## A locked top five with a shuffling order

The **same five tools — monday, Smartsheet, Asana, ClickUp, Jira — have been top-5 every single week for 10 weeks.** What changes is who's on top:

Smartsheet → monday → ClickUp → Smartsheet → Smartsheet → monday → monday → monday → monday → **monday**

Three tools have held #1 (monday 6, Smartsheet 3, ClickUp 1), but **monday has led the last five weeks.** Their 10-week averages are tightly bunched (monday 97.7, ClickUp 93.9, Smartsheet 92.9, Asana 88.9).

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of Project Management recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4, US region) and records which brands are named and how prominently.

- **DECA Score** is a 0–100 index of recommendation strength within a category.
- **Data window:** 10 weeks, May 10 – July 12, 2026. Rankings reflect the July 12 week.
- **Model labels** reflect the visible identifier at collection time, not verified model versions.
- **Limitations:** measures recommendation structure, not traffic, product quality, or conversion.
