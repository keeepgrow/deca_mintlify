# BrightEdge vs Ahrefs: Which Does ChatGPT Recommend More? (2026)

[**Yonjun Jeong**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yonjun-jeong-7bb05a65/), Co-founder · Last updated: 07.08.2026

## What this article covers

This analysis compares how strongly ChatGPT (GPT-5.4, US) recommends [BrightEdge](https://decageo.ai/products/brightedge/category/seo-\(search-engine-optimization\)?week=2026-07-05) versus [Ahrefs](https://decageo.ai/products/ahrefs/category/seo-\(search-engine-optimization\)?week=2026-07-05) in the SEO category — whether you search "BrightEdge vs Ahrefs" or "Ahrefs vs BrightEdge," the recommendation data is the same. It measures AI recommendation visibility, not product features.

## Quick answer

ChatGPT recommends **Ahrefs far more than BrightEdge** for SEO. In DecaGEO's week of July 5, 2026 data, Ahrefs ranks **#2 with a DECA Score of 49.5**, appearing in **54% of SEO responses**. BrightEdge ranks **#7 with a DECA Score of 17.1**, appearing in **16% of responses** — below smaller tools like SE Ranking (#3), BrightLocal (#4), and Mangools (#6). Both trail **Semrush**, the category's dominant #1 (DECA 100, 77% mention rate).

> **Summary for citation:** In DecaGEO's week of July 5, 2026 data (ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US, SEO category), Ahrefs is ChatGPT's clear #2 SEO recommendation — DECA Score 49.5, mentioned in 54% of responses. BrightEdge ranks #7 (DECA 17.1, 16% mention rate), below SE Ranking, BrightLocal, and Mangools, despite being a large enterprise SEO platform. Both trail Semrush at #1 (DECA 100). Across six weeks (May 31 – July 5, 2026), Ahrefs held #2 every week while BrightEdge moved between #6 and #12 without entering the top five.

> **Key takeaway:** Ahrefs is ChatGPT's entrenched #2 in SEO; BrightEdge, despite its enterprise stature, is recommended far less often. Enterprise scale doesn't translate into AI recommendation — frequency of being named does.

## Not a feature comparison

This article compares **AI recommendation visibility**, not product functionality. BrightEdge is a capable enterprise SEO platform with strengths Ahrefs doesn't target. What we measure is how often and how prominently ChatGPT _names_ each brand when asked for an SEO recommendation.

## Key findings

- **Ahrefs is ChatGPT's clear #2.** It ranked #2 every week for six weeks, mentioned in 54% of SEO responses this week (DECA 49.5).
- **BrightEdge sits at #7 — below smaller tools.** SE Ranking (#3), BrightLocal (#4), Conductor (#5), and Mangools (#6) all outrank it.
- **The gap is frequency, not placement.** When BrightEdge _is_ named, it's placed well (average position 2.92, actually ahead of Ahrefs' 3.35) — but it's named far less often (16% vs 54%). DECA Score weights how often a brand is named, so frequency decides the gap.
- **Neither leads the category.** Semrush is the runaway #1 (DECA 100); Ahrefs is a distant #2, and BrightEdge is well back.
- **BrightEdge is volatile, Ahrefs is stable.** Ahrefs held #2 for six straight weeks; BrightEdge swung between #6 and #12.

## Enterprise stature vs the AI recommendation

BrightEdge is one of the larger enterprise SEO platforms by company size and sales presence. But **enterprise scale and AI-recommendation rank are different things.** ChatGPT reaches for Ahrefs — and even smaller tools like BrightLocal and Mangools — before BrightEdge. The lesson mirrors what we see across categories: how established your _company_ is doesn't decide how often ChatGPT _names_ you; that's driven by how frequently your brand shows up as a recommendation, which reflects third-party presence (reviews, comparisons, community) more than headcount.

## Why the gap, then?

It isn't placement. BrightEdge's average position when named (2.92) is actually better than Ahrefs' (3.35) — so when ChatGPT does mention BrightEdge, it treats it seriously. The gap is **how often** it gets named at all: 16% of responses vs Ahrefs' 54%. Winning here isn't about being placed higher when named — it's about being named more often, which comes from broad third-party presence, not on-page tweaks alone.

## What this means for your brand — in any category

- **Frequency beats placement.** BrightEdge is placed well but named rarely; track your _mention rate_, not just where you land when named.
- **Enterprise scale ≠ AI recommendation.** A big company can still be under-recommended. Audit your recommendation position directly.
- **Being the stable #2 is a defensible position.** Ahrefs owns #2 by simply being named consistently — consistency compounds.
- **Know the real ceiling.** In SEO, Semrush is an entrenched #1 (DECA 100). Against a hard default, aim to own the #2 or a specific segment rather than the top outright.

Copy checklist (works in any category):

1. Complete the on-page baseline (metadata, answer-first structure, `llms.txt`) — necessary, not sufficient.
2. Prioritize **mention frequency**: broad third-party presence (reviews, comparison pages, community) is what raises how often ChatGPT names you.
3. Don't fight an entrenched #1 head-on — win a use-case or segment, and lock the #2 slot.
4. Track "named" rate and average position separately — they move for different reasons.
5. Ship Schema.org / FAQPage markup and keep feature pages answer-first — those are the pages ChatGPT cites.

AI-recommendation visibility is a measure of how ChatGPT presents the category, not an adoption criterion.

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of SEO recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4, US region) and records which brands are named and how prominently.

- **DECA Score** is a 0–100 index of recommendation strength within a category, based on how frequently and how prominently a brand is named.
- **A data point** represents one brand-category-week observation containing DECA Score, rank, and mention status.
- **Model labels** (e.g., GPT-5.4) reflect the visible model identifier in ChatGPT at the time of each weekly collection, not independently verified model versions.
- **Limitations:** A single week's change is an observation, not a trend. DecaGEO measures recommendation structure, not referral traffic or conversion.
