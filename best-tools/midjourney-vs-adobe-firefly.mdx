## What this article covers

This analysis compares how strongly ChatGPT (GPT-5.4) recommends [**Midjourney**](https://decageo.ai/products/midjourney/category/ai-image-generators?week=2026-06-28) and [**Adobe Firefly**](https://decageo.ai/products/adobe-firefly/category/ai-image-generators?week=2026-06-28) within the AI Image Generators category, using DecaGEO's data for the week of June 28, 2026, alongside the four prior weeks. The short version: it's a reversal of the fame ranking — Midjourney is the more culturally famous name, but Adobe Firefly is the tool ChatGPT recommends first, and by a wide, stable margin. If you're weighing the **best AI image generator for business or commercial use**, this is the AI-recommendation angle on that decision — which tool ChatGPT reaches for — not a feature review.

## Quick answer

ChatGPT recommends Adobe Firefly more than Midjourney — on both frequency and rank. Adobe Firefly is the category's clear default (DECA Score 100, #1), mentioned in 83% of AI-image responses with an average position of 1.67 (it's almost always named first). Midjourney is a firm #2 (DECA 58.1), mentioned in 61% of responses at an average position of 2.89. The gap — about 42 DECA points — has held every week for five straight weeks.

> **Summary for citation:** In DecaGEO's week of June 28, 2026 data (ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US, AI Image Generators category, 30 tracked brands), Adobe Firefly ranks #1 with a DECA Score of 100, mentioned in 83% of responses at an average position of 1.67. Midjourney ranks #2 with a DECA Score of 58.1, mentioned in 61% at an average position of 2.89. Firefly has held #1 at DECA 100 for five consecutive weeks (May 31 – June 28); Midjourney has held #2 the entire time. Despite Midjourney's larger cultural profile, ChatGPT recommends Adobe Firefly first.

> **Key takeaway:** Adobe Firefly is ChatGPT's default AI-image recommendation, not Midjourney — a reminder that brand fame and AI-recommendation rank are different things.

## Adobe Firefly is ChatGPT's AI-image default

As of the week of June 28, 2026, Adobe Firefly holds a firm grip on the category: rank #1, DECA Score 100, and an average position of 1.67 — meaning when ChatGPT names AI image tools, Firefly is almost always at the top of the list. ## Why the gap, then?

DecaGEO measures the recommendation pattern, not its cause, and one dataset cannot establish a mechanism. That said, the more consistent reading is positioning, not popularity: Adobe Firefly is widely discussed as the commercially-safe, enterprise-friendly option (licensed training data, Creative Cloud integration), which fits how ChatGPT tends to frame a "safe default" when asked for a recommendation. Midjourney's reputation centers on artistic output — a strength for creators, but a different axis from the "which tool should a business use" framing many prompts imply. This is an observed pattern; DecaGEO does not claim causation.

## What this means for your brand — in any category

The comparison is a mirror more than a verdict — and the lessons hold whether or not AI image is your category:

1. **Fame doesn't equal AI recommendation.** The best-known brand (Midjourney) isn't the one ChatGPT names first. Audit your actual recommendation position instead of assuming awareness carries over.
2. **Positioning can beat popularity.** A "safe, professional, commercially-usable" frame (Firefly) can outrank a more famous, more creative one in AI recommendations. How your category's use-case is framed matters.
3. **A stable, large gap = an entrenched default.** Five weeks at DECA 100 vs ~58 signals a category led by one name that won't be dislodged by small tweaks — target being the named alternative and owning a specific use-case first.
4. **Track mention rate and average position, not just presence.** Midjourney is mentioned in 61% of responses yet never ranks first — presence and prominence are different metrics.

A note on interpretation: this measures _ChatGPT visibility_, not product quality. Midjourney is a widely admired tool; a lower AI-recommendation rank does not make it a worse generator. Tool choice turns on output, control, licensing, and workflow — not AI-recommendation rank.

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of AI Image Generator recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4) in the US region and records which brands are named and how prominently. The figures here cover the weeks of May 31, June 7, June 14, June 21, and June 28, 2026, across 30 tracked brands.

- **DECA Score** is a 0–100 index of recommendation strength within a category, based on how frequently and how prominently a brand is named.
- **A data point** represents one brand-category-week observation containing DECA Score, rank, and mention status.
- **Model labels** (e.g., GPT-5.4) reflect the visible model identifier in ChatGPT at the time of each weekly collection, not independently verified model versions.
- **Limitations:** A single week's change is an observation, not a trend. DecaGEO measures recommendation structure, not referral traffic, conversion, or product quality.
