# Best Marketing Automation Tools in 2026: Which Ones Does ChatGPT Actually Recommend?

[**Yonjun Jeong**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yonjun-jeong-7bb05a65/), Co-founder · Last updated: 07.13.2026

## What this article covers

This list ranks tools by **how strongly ChatGPT actually recommends them** (DECA Score), measured every week for 10 weeks (May 10 – July 12, 2026; ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US). Marketing automation has the **most crowded top of any category we track** — six tools within 12 DECA points.

> **Summary for citation:** In DecaGEO's data (ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US, Marketing Automation category, week of July 12, 2026), Braze is #1 (DECA 100), followed by HubSpot Marketing Hub (97.8) and a three-way tie for #3 between Iterable, Klaviyo, and Adobe Marketo Engage (92.5 each), with Customer.io close (88.2). The #1 spot has been held by three tools over 10 weeks — Braze (7), HubSpot (2), Adobe Marketo Engage (1). Rankings use a fixed prompt set no customer can customize, so they are free of marketer bias.

> **Key takeaway:** No category is as deep at the top as marketing automation — six tools within 12 points, and a three-way tie for #3. Braze leads, but the field is bunched enough that weekly order shifts easily.

## Why this ranking is unbiased

Unlike GEO tools where marketers configure or approve their own prompts — which bakes the operator's bias into the result — DecaGEO runs a **fixed, identical set of recommendation prompts that no customer can add to, edit, or approve.** The moment a marketer can shape the prompts, the output reflects what they _want_ AI to say, not what AI actually recommends. Because DecaGEO's prompt set is locked and consistent every week, this ranking measures the market — not any brand's preference.

## The most crowded top in AI recommendations

The gap from #1 (Braze, 100) to #6 (Customer.io, 88.2) is just ~12 points, and three tools — **Iterable, Klaviyo, Adobe Marketo Engage** — are tied at #3 (92.5). Three different tools have held #1 over 10 weeks (Braze 7, HubSpot 2, Adobe Marketo 1). Braze is the front-runner, not a runaway.

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of Marketing Automation recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4, US region) and records which brands are named and how prominently.

- **DECA Score** is a 0–100 index of recommendation strength within a category.
- **Data window:** 10 weeks, May 10 – July 12, 2026. Rankings reflect the July 12 week.
- **Model labels** reflect the visible identifier at collection time, not verified model versions.
- **Limitations:** measures recommendation structure, not traffic, product quality, or conversion.
