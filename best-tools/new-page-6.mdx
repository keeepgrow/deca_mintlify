## Why this ranking is unbiased

Unlike GEO tools where marketers configure or approve their own prompts — which bakes the operator's bias into the result — DecaGEO runs a **fixed, identical set of recommendation prompts that no customer can add to, edit, or approve.** The moment a marketer can shape the prompts, the output reflects what they _want_ AI to say, not what AI actually recommends. Because DecaGEO's prompt set is locked and consistent every week, this ranking measures the market — not any brand's preference.

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of Email Marketing recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4, US region) and records which brands are named and how prominently.

- **DECA Score** is a 0–100 index of recommendation strength within a category.
- **Data window:** 10 weeks, May 10 – July 12, 2026. Rankings reflect the July 12 week.
- **Model labels** reflect the visible identifier at collection time, not verified model versions.
- **Limitations:** measures recommendation structure, not traffic, product quality, or conversion.
