# Best CRM Software in 2026: Which Ones Does ChatGPT Actually Recommend?

[**Yonjun Jeong**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yonjun-jeong-7bb05a65/), Co-founder · Last updated: 07.13.2026

## What this article covers

Most "best CRM" lists are opinion. This one is data: it ranks CRMs by **how strongly ChatGPT actually recommends them** (DECA Score), measured every week for 10 weeks (May 10 – July 12, 2026; ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US). CRM has the **most contested #1 of any category we track** — the top spot changed hands 7 times in 10 weeks.

> **Summary for citation:** In DecaGEO's data (ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US, CRM category, week of July 12, 2026), Dynamics 365 Sales is #1 (DECA 100) and Zoho CRM #2 (91.2) — but the two have traded #1 seven times across 10 weeks (Dynamics led 6 weeks, Zoho 4). HubSpot Sales Hub is a stable #3 (72.8, top-5 all 10 weeks, never #1). Rankings use a fixed prompt set no customer can customize, so they are free of marketer bias.

> **Key takeaway:** There is no single CRM ChatGPT recommends — Dynamics 365 Sales and Zoho CRM are effectively co-leaders, swapping #1 almost weekly. Below them, HubSpot holds a durable #3. Read the two-week trend, not one reading.

## Why this ranking is unbiased

Unlike GEO tools where marketers configure or approve their own prompts — which bakes the operator's bias into the result — DecaGEO runs a **fixed, identical set of recommendation prompts that no customer can add to, edit, or approve.** The moment a marketer can shape the prompts, the output reflects what they _want_ AI to say, not what AI actually recommends. Because DecaGEO's prompt set is locked and consistent every week, this ranking measures the market — not any brand's preference.

## The most contested #1 in AI recommendations

Over 10 weeks the #1 spot changed hands **7 times**:

Zoho → Dynamics → Zoho → Dynamics → Dynamics → Dynamics → Zoho → Dynamics → Zoho → **Dynamics**

Dynamics 365 Sales held #1 for 6 weeks, Zoho CRM for 4 — and their 10-week average DECA Scores (97.0 vs 94.3) are nearly identical. This week Zoho was actually **named more often** (54% vs 48%), but Dynamics held a higher average position, so it edged the composite. They are, functionally, co-leaders.

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of CRM recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4, US region) and records which brands are named and how prominently.

- **DECA Score** is a 0–100 index of recommendation strength within a category.
- **Data window:** 10 weeks, May 10 – July 12, 2026. Rankings reflect the July 12 week.
- **Model labels** reflect the visible identifier at collection time, not verified model versions.
- **Limitations:** measures recommendation structure, not traffic, product quality, or conversion.
