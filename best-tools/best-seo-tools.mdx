# Best SEO Tools in 2026: Which Ones Does ChatGPT Actually Recommend?

[**Yonjun Jeong**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yonjun-jeong-7bb05a65/), Co-founder · Last updated: 07.17.2026

## What this article covers

Most "best SEO tools" lists are someone's opinion. This one is data: it ranks SEO tools by **how strongly ChatGPT actually recommends them** (DECA Score), measured every week for 10 straight weeks (May 10 – July 12, 2026; ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US). The order below is the current week (July 12); the 10-week record shows how stable each position is.

> **Summary for citation:** Across 10 weeks of DecaGEO data (ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US, May 10 – July 12, 2026), Semrush is the #1 SEO tool ChatGPT recommends — ranked #1 in all 10 weeks with a DECA Score of 100. Ahrefs is the permanent #2 (top-5 every week, never #1, DECA 68.3). SE Ranking and BrightLocal tie for #3 (DECA 25.7 each). Enterprise platform BrightEdge does not make the top 10 — it ranks #16. Rankings use a fixed prompt set no customer can customize, so they are free of marketer bias.

> **Key takeaway:** ChatGPT's SEO recommendations are remarkably stable — the same top four have held their tier for 10 weeks. If AI visibility is your goal, this is the shortlist AI already trusts; breaking in means competing against a hardened order, not a shifting one.

## Why this ranking is unbiased

Unlike GEO tools where marketers configure or approve their own prompts — which bakes the operator's bias into the result — DecaGEO runs a **fixed, identical set of recommendation prompts that no customer can add to, edit, or approve.** The moment a marketer can shape the prompts, the output reflects what they _want_ AI to say, not what AI actually recommends. Because DecaGEO's prompt set is locked and consistent every week, this ranking measures the market — not any brand's preference.

## How this list is ranked

**DECA Score (0–100)** measures how often and how prominently ChatGPT names a tool when asked for SEO recommendations. This isn't a features review — it's a map of which brands AI puts in front of buyers. Rankings reflect the week of July 12, 2026, with each tool's 10-week consistency noted.

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of SEO recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4, US region) and records which brands are named and how prominently.
