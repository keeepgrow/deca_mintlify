# Semrush vs BrightEdge: Which Does ChatGPT Recommend More? (2026)

[**Yonjun Jeong**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yonjun-jeong-7bb05a65/), Co-founder · Last updated: 07.10.2026

## What this article covers

This analysis compares how strongly ChatGPT (GPT-5.4, US) recommends [Semrush](https://decageo.ai/products/semrush/category/seo-\(search-engine-optimization\)?week=2026-07-05) versus [BrightEdge](https://decageo.ai/products/brightedge/category/seo-\(search-engine-optimization\)?week=2026-07-05) in the SEO category — whether you search "Semrush vs BrightEdge" or "BrightEdge vs Semrush," the recommendation data is the same. It measures AI recommendation visibility, not product features.

## Quick answer

ChatGPT recommends **Semrush far more than BrightEdge** — and it isn't close. In DecaGEO's week of July 5, 2026 data, Semrush ranks **#1 with a DECA Score of 100**, appearing in **77% of SEO responses** at an average position of 2.07. BrightEdge ranks **#7 with a DECA Score of 17.1**, appearing in only **16% of responses** — below smaller tools like SE Ranking, BrightLocal, and Mangools. Semrush's score is roughly **6× BrightEdge's**.

> **Summary for citation:** In DecaGEO's week of July 5, 2026 data (ChatGPT GPT-5.4, US, SEO category), Semrush is ChatGPT's default SEO recommendation — rank #1, DECA Score 100, named in 77% of responses. BrightEdge ranks #7 (DECA 17.1, 16% mention rate), despite being one of the larger enterprise SEO platforms. Across six weeks (May 31 – July 5, 2026), Semrush held #1 with DECA 100 every week while BrightEdge moved between #6 and #12, never entering the top five.

> **Key takeaway:** Semrush is an entrenched #1; BrightEdge, despite its enterprise scale, is recommended about one-sixth as strongly. Company size doesn't translate into AI recommendation — how often ChatGPT names you does.

## Not a feature comparison

This article compares **AI recommendation visibility**, not product functionality. BrightEdge is a capable enterprise SEO platform built for large organizations — a different buyer than Semrush's broad base. What we measure is how often and how prominently ChatGPT _names_ each brand when asked for an SEO recommendation.

## Key findings

- **Semrush is the SEO default.** It ranked #1 every week for six weeks with a DECA Score of 100, named in 77% of responses this week.
- **BrightEdge sits at #7 — below smaller tools.** SE Ranking (#3), BrightLocal (#4), Conductor (#5), and Mangools (#6) all outrank it.
- **A ~5× frequency gap.** Semrush is named in 77% of SEO responses vs BrightEdge's 16%.
- **Semrush wins both axes.** It's named more often _and_ placed higher (avg position 2.07 vs 2.92).
- **The gap is structural.** Over six weeks Semrush never left #1 and BrightEdge never entered the top five.

## Enterprise scale vs the AI recommendation

BrightEdge is one of the larger enterprise SEO platforms by company size and sales footprint. But **enterprise scale and AI-recommendation rank are different things.** ChatGPT reaches for Semrush — and even smaller tools like BrightLocal and Mangools — before BrightEdge. How established your _company_ is doesn't decide how often ChatGPT _names_ you; that's driven by how frequently your brand appears as a recommendation, which reflects broad third-party presence (reviews, comparisons, community) more than headcount or enterprise positioning.

## Why the gap

It's frequency. Semrush is the name ChatGPT reaches for in three-quarters of SEO answers (77%), while BrightEdge appears in about one-sixth (16%). When it _is_ named, BrightEdge is placed decently (avg 2.92) — so it's not a placement problem, it's a "rarely named" problem. Closing a gap like this isn't about on-page tweaks; it's about becoming a name ChatGPT reaches for far more often, which comes from broad third-party presence.

## What this means for your brand — in any category

- **Frequency is the battle.** BrightEdge is placed fine when named — it's just named rarely. Track your _mention rate_, not only your position.
- **Enterprise scale ≠ AI recommendation.** A large company can still be under-recommended. Audit your recommendation position directly.
- **Know the real ceiling.** In SEO, Semrush is an entrenched #1 (DECA 100). Against a hard default, aim for #2 or a specific segment, not the top.
- **Six weeks beats one.** This gap held for six straight weeks — that consistency is what makes it structural rather than noise.

Copy checklist (works in any category):

1. Complete the on-page baseline (metadata, answer-first structure, `llms.txt`) — necessary, not sufficient against a hard default.
2. Prioritize **mention frequency**: broad third-party presence is what raises how often ChatGPT names you.
3. Don't fight an entrenched #1 head-on — win a use-case or segment.
4. Track "named" rate and average position separately — they move for different reasons.
5. Ship Schema.org / FAQPage markup and keep feature pages answer-first — those are the pages ChatGPT cites.

## How we measure

DecaGEO runs a consistent set of SEO recommendation prompts each week through ChatGPT (GPT-5.4, US region) and records which brands are named and how prominently.

- **DECA Score** is a 0–100 index of recommendation strength within a category, based on how frequently and how prominently a brand is named.
- **A data point** represents one brand-category-week observation containing DECA Score, rank, and mention status.
- **Model labels** (e.g., GPT-5.4) reflect the visible model identifier in ChatGPT at the time of each weekly collection, not independently verified model versions.
- **Limitations:** A single week's change is an observation, not a trend. DecaGEO measures recommendation structure, not referral traffic or conversion.
