<Update label="July 9, 2026" tags={["New releases","Improvements"]}>
  ## See who's breaking into the AI chart — and copy how they did it

  The new **Dashboard** turns each week's chart movement into a plan you can act on. Sign in and open it from the header to see who just broke into your category this week, what they did to get there, and the exact moves you can copy.

  ### What to watch this week

  Your Dashboard opens to a weekly view of who's moving on the chart, so you see the week's breakouts at a glance:

  - **New Entries** — products landing on the chart for the first time.
  - **Top Movers** — already-ranked products climbing the ranks on real gains of their own, not just because rivals dropped off.

  Each comes with its Rank, Mention Rate, and a one-line summary of what changed. Switch category or week anytime.

  ### What they did

  Go past the "who" to the "what." DecaGEO finds the shared pattern behind this week's breakouts, then breaks it down product by product:

  - **Off-Page Citations** — the new third-party sources AI cites when it mentions them, grouped by domain type.
  - **On-Page GEO Patterns** — the on-page signals most of this week's breakouts newly adopted.

  That's the repeatable play behind the movement — the part you can copy.

  ### Your action plan

  Turn the pattern into steps. Your Action Plan has two parts:

  - **Category Basic** — the fundamentals almost every top-ranked product already has in place, ordered by how common each one is, so you close the most widespread gaps first.
  - **Weekly Playbook** — this week's breakout moves, ranked by impact and effort, so you start where the payoff is biggest for the least work.

  Every action is ready to run: 

  - Copy its **AI prompt**, paste in your own page URL or content, and AI applies the play for you — the exact rewrite, tag, or markup it calls for. No guesswork.

  You get a first look for free. Pro unlocks the full checklist and every weekly play — see [Pricing](https://decageo.ai/pricing).

  ### Improvements

  - After you **Add Your Brand**, the confirmation now sends you straight to your Dashboard to see this week's breakouts — or, if you don't have an account yet, to create a free one and open it.
</Update>

<Update label="June 17, 2026" tags={["New releases","Improvements"]}>
  ## Search for your brand and see where you stand

  No more scrolling to find yourself. Search any brand or domain from the new landing page and DecaGEO tells you whether it's ranked — and takes you straight to the answer.

  ### Search any brand or domain

  Search a brand name or website on the new landing page (or from the header), and you'll land on a results page that shows whether it's ranked. From there, open its category chart or its product detail page.

  Not ranked yet? See how similar brands recently broke in, and Add Your Brand or Request a Chart on the spot.

  ### See exactly where a brand stands

  Open a ranked brand's category chart from your search and its row is highlighted, with a card up top showing its current rank, DECA Score, and Mention Rate plus a shortcut to full details — so you spot its position without losing the full ranking.

  ### At a glance

  Every category chart now opens with three quick signals — the week's **Leader**, the biggest **Top Mover**, and the newest **New Entry** — so you get the story at a glance.

  ### Improvements

  - **Add Your Brand** is more reliable: it catches duplicate submissions — pointing you to **Settings → Brand** to check or edit instead of adding the same brand twice — and clearly confirms when a brand is submitted or updated.
</Update>

<Update label="May 29, 2026" tags={["New releases","Improvements"]}>
  ## Rankings now go product by product — and show you why

  DecaGEO now ranks individual **products**, not just the companies that make them. Each product gets a full detail page with brand-new analysis, you can track the ones you care about, and a new Pro plan unlocks the complete picture.

  ### Product-level charts

  Charts now rank products, not just companies — so each of a company's products stands on its own instead of being lumped under one parent brand. You finally compare the things buyers actually choose between.

  ### Brand Hub

  Every company now has a Brand Hub: one place to see all its products, the categories they show up in, and how they're trending.

  ### A rebuilt product detail page

  The old brand page is now a full product detail page that brings everything together:

  - **Overview** — a product's rank, momentum, and strongest segments at a glance, with a jump to the detail behind any of them.
  - **Chart History** — a product's DECA Score and Mention Rate week over week (last 4 weeks free; 8-week and full-history views on Pro).
  - **Segment Position** — your rank and mention rate in every buyer segment (business scale, use case, channel), color-coded so you spot where a product is strongest and weakest at a glance.
  - **Off-Page Citations** — which third-party sources AI cites when it mentions a product, broken down by domain type and benchmarked against the category, plus which domains cite you and which don't — so you can see where to earn coverage next.
  - **On-Page GEO Patterns** — see what a product focuses on on-page to win AI mentions: the content, metadata, and technical patterns its most-cited pages emphasize, with those pages as evidence — so you can benchmark its approach and bring it to your own. (Pro)
  - **Prompt & AI Response** — the actual buyer-style prompts and AI answers behind the rankings.

  ### Track the products you care about

  Bookmark the products that matter and watch them together on the new **Tracked Products** page — each with its rank and week-over-week change. Track 1 product free, unlimited on Pro.

  ### Add Your Brand

  Not ranked yet? Register your brand from **\+ Add Your Brand** in the header, and we'll email you the moment it lands on a chart — with an in-app notification too if you're signed in.

  ### Introducing Pro

  Most of DecaGEO stays free. **Pro (\$49/month)** unlocks the full picture — complete history, every citation, On-Page GEO Patterns, and unlimited tracking with exports. See what's included on [Pricing](https://decageo.ai/pricing).

  ### Improvements

  - Category charts now show filters by default — filter by Market Segment without opening a panel first.
  - Search for a brand directly inside any category chart.
</Update>

<Update label="April 17, 2026" tags={["New releases"]} description="DecaGEO 1.0">
  ## **DecaGEO is live**

  DecaGEO is the first standardized ranking system for **AI brand visibility**. It asks\
  AI the same kinds of questions real buyers ask, then turns the answers into a\
  weekly, standardized ranking — so you can finally measure where you stand in AI's responses.

  ### What's included

  - **Weekly brand visibility rankings** across 10 SaaS categories, covering 100\+ brands.
    - **DECA Score (0–100)** — how frequently and prominently AI recommends each brand.
    - **Mention Rate** — the share of AI responses that mention a brand.
    - **Market Segment filters** — slice rankings by the conditions that matter in each category.
  - **Brand details** — performance by category, trends over time, and the actual AI responses behind the numbers.
  - **Request a Chart** — don't see your market? Ask us to add it.

  ### **Coverage at launch**

  - **AI Platform**: ChatGPT (GPT-5.4)
  - **Market**: United States, English
  - **Categories**: 10 SaaS verticals — CRM, Project Management, Help Desk, Marketing Automation, Email Marketing, Influencer Marketing Platforms, SEO, GEO(Generative Engine Optimization), AI Writing Assistants, and AI Image Generators.

  ### How it works

  - Persona-driven prompts that simulate real buyer decision scenarios
  - Recommendation-only counting — passing mentions and negative references are excluded.
  - A fixed model and weekly collection window, so every week is comparable to the last.

  [Read the full methodology →](https://decageo.ai/methodology)
</Update>
