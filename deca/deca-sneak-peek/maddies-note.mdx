## April 17, 2026

### We Shipped It.

Today we launched DecaGEO MVP.

10 SaaS categories. 100\+ brands ranked. Weekly updates. One question answered: "Where does your brand stand in AI recommendations?"

Three months ago, we were a GEO content creation tool. We had users. We had revenue. But the more we talked to marketers, the more we heard the same thing:

"I don't even know if AI mentions us."

Not "help me write content." Not "optimize my pages." Just: "Does ChatGPT recommend us? Where do we rank?"

Nobody had the answer. Not the SEO tools. Not the AI visibility checkers. Nobody was measuring this consistently, with a fixed methodology, week over week.

So we pivoted.

We stopped building a writing tool and started building a measurement standard. DECA Score. Mention Rate. Rank. Three numbers that tell you exactly where your brand stands in the AI recommendation economy.

Is this the right call? Ask me in six months.

But today, it's live. And for the first time, brands can see where they actually stand when someone asks AI "what should I use?"
