# Work with DECA Agent

After creating a project, DECA starts a conversation to guide you through content creation. You talk, DECA proposes next steps, and you decide what to do.

## How It Works

DECA works like a strategic partner, not a one-shot generator:

1. **DECA analyzes** - Reviews your project status
2. **DECA proposes** - Suggests what to do next with reasoning
3. **You decide** - Choose an option or ask for something else
4. **DECA delivers** - Creates the work and shows results

### Example Conversation

```
DECA: "Brand Research is complete. I recommend Persona Analysis 
to identify high-value Target Prompts. Shall we start?"

You: "Yes, analyze persona"

DECA: "I've identified 3 key personas. Which should we analyze first?
1. The Overwhelmed Solo Entrepreneur
2. The Growth-Hacking Marketing Manager
3. The CRM & Retention Specialist"

You: "Option 1"

DECA: [Creates detailed persona analysis with Target Prompts]
```

## The Workflow

DECA guides you through four stages:

- **Brand Research** - Understands your positioning and voice
- **Persona Analysis** - Finds Target Prompts your audience asks AI
- **Content Strategy** - Plans topics using Pillar-Cluster structure
- **Content Drafts** - Writes citation-ready content

You can follow DECA's recommendations or jump to any stage using the step buttons.

## Why DECA Is Different

**Talk naturally** Use DECA like ChatGPT or Gemini. Ask for content, upload files, request changes—it works conversationally.

**But DECA knows GEO** DECA already understands your brand, Target Prompts, and citation optimization. You don't need to explain GEO principles—DECA applies them automatically.

**See everything (White Box)** DECA shows every step: finding files, searching sources, evaluating drafts. Nothing happens in a black box.

## Tips

- Be casual: "Make content" works fine—DECA will ask if it needs more info
- Upload files: Add docs, PDFs, or images for more context
- Refine anytime: Say "make it casual" or "add data" to iterate
- Switch languages: Korean and English work interchangeably

## Next Steps

Your first conversation usually starts with Persona Analysis, then moves to Strategy and Drafts. DECA will guide you, but you can always go back and refine anything.

Ready? Just respond to DECA's message and start building your GEO content.
