# Plans & Pricing

## Free During Launch

DecaGEO is currently **free to use**. Browse AI brand visibility rankings across all available categories at no cost.

## What You Get

- **Full ranking access.** Browse weekly-updated AI brand visibility charts across all 10 SaaS categories. See DECA Scores, Mention Rates, and Rankings for 100\+ brands.
- **Browse freely, sign up for full access.** Basic rankings are visible to everyone. Sign up for free to unlock detailed DECA Scores, Mention Rates, and weekly update alerts.
- **Weekly updates.** Rankings refresh every week with new data collected from AI platforms.
