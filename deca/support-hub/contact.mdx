# Contact Us

Have questions, feedback, or partnership ideas? We'd love to hear from you.

## Email

[support@decageo.ai](mailto:support@decageo.ai)

You can reach us about:

- General questions about DecaGEO
- Methodology and ranking inquiries
- Category requests (want us to track a new category?)
- Partnership and collaboration proposals
- Bug reports or technical issues

We aim to respond within 1 business day.

## What to Include

To help us assist you efficiently, please include:

- Your name or company name
- A clear description of your question or request
- For technical issues: screenshots and steps to reproduce
